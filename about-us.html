About Us

Bold Billiards is a company that sells different equipment that is required for playing billiards such as cues, tables, etc. It also offers the service of billiards tournaments on the weekends. Bold Billiards has been operating for the last 5 years but has only decided to make a website now. We have 4 different employees.
